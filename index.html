Welcome to Rajasthan Technical University

University currently affiliates about 68 Engineering Colleges, 03 B.Arch, 16 MCA Colleges, 39 MBA Colleges, 31 M.Tech Colleges, 01 M.Arch and 01 Hotel Management and Catering Institute

Rajasthan Technical University

RTU was established in 2006 by the Government of Rajasthan to enhance the technical education in the state.

Our Vison

To achieve academic excellence in Engineering by imparting in depth
to the students,facilitating research activities and cater to the ever changing industrial demands, global and societal needs.

Our Mission

To provide quality engineering education to the students To offer state of arts education in engineering.To establish

industry institute interaction to make students ready for the

industria To promote research based projects/activities

to develop department as Center of Excellence for Research.
